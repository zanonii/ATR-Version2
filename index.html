Come Out And Play! As Album To Remember (ATR) is home to tech savvy photographers, artists and general free thinkers I decided to make my first game based on you. This version is a variation of 2048 with a few modifications of the President 2048 version that is demonstrated in the Udacity video. The original 2048 can be found on a link below and can be played on a number of mobile platforms; check Playstore or other providers for your phone apps for the original 2048

Make your own 2048. No programming experience is required and in less than half an hour you'll dig into the source code, make some changes, and have your own version of the game to share.

How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same value touch, they merge into one!

This is a modified version of the original game created by Gabriele Cirulli.
